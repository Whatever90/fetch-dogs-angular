Welcome to Dog Adoption Hub

This site is a demo project created to help dog lovers explore shelter dogs and find a potential new friend. Here, you can search by breed, view detailed dog profiles, mark your favorites, and even generate a match based on your preferences. Please note that this website is a personal, non-commercial project built for demonstration and educational purposes. While we strive to create a seamless and enjoyable browsing experience, some features or data may be simulated and are not part of an official dog adoption service. Enjoy exploring and thank you for visiting!
